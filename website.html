Visualizations of Call Center Data









As we might expect, we see that call volumes are highest from 9:00AM to 4:00PM on weekdays. Interestingly, call volume also seems to spike around 10:00PM-12:00PM on Saturdays




Here we see that the ratio of complaints to non-complaints remains relatively consistent with a slightly lower percentage of complaints from August to October. Also, in terms of total volume of complaints and non-complaints, there seems to be on average a higher number of 311 calls from the first 6 months of the year compared to the last 6 months of the year.




It seems like the time of the day and day of the year may affect whether a call is a complaint or not. See our Jupyter notebook to run our logistic regression model by choosing a certain time of day and certain day of year to predict whether or not a call will be a complaint.
